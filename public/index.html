Suburble Melbourne

How To Play

Surburble Melbourne is a worlde-esque game that involves guessing a specific Melbourne suburb that refreshes each day.

Each guess is accompanied by some demographic and geographic data for that suburb, as well as a comparison to the hidden/target suburb based on these stats. A means the hidden suburb has the same value for that category.

In this example, the hidden suburb is less than 7km from the CBD in a south-west direction, has a population of more than 9,368, a median age less than 38, a median income higher than 70k, and a median rent of $421.

Data sourced from openstreetmap.org and the 2021 ABS census.
